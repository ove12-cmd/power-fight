What is a Private Booking?

Private sessions give you direct, focused coaching without the group schedule. Whether you're working on a specific technique, preparing for competition, or simply prefer 1-on-1 attention — we build the session around you.

  • Full coach attention every minute of the session
  • Choose your discipline: striking, grappling, S&C, or MMA
  • Flexible scheduling outside of regular class times
  • Open to all levels — beginner to competitive fighter
